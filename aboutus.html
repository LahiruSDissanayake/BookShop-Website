Mr.Lucifer Morningstar

Public Bookstore (Pvt) Ltd is a division of the David Group of Companies in Sri Lanka. The bookshop first commenced business over 65 years ago in the rural township of kotte. In 1973, it moved to a busier location and found its permanent home in Nugegoda. Within just a short period of time Public Bookstore expanded operations and activities in a network of Branches Island wide to better serve its customers. Today we are comprised of more than 20 branches including bookstores in some of the major cities in Sri Lanka such as Kandy, Matara, Gampaha and Colombo.

David Publishers (Pvt) Ltd which was established in 2000 is another subsidiary of the David Group of Companies. It is the publishing and printing arm of David, publishing over 100 titles per year.
